About Catboy and the Rule of Engagement

A Brief History into Catboy

Who is Catboy? Well he is a someone my great nephew thinks he is from time to time raising his arms in such a way and charging towards various members of his family. It is because of this boy of 2 nearly 3 years old character that I decided to invent a fantasy world around the keywords he has picked up and named additional characters to be pitted into a game of cards.

The Characters and a brief History in Alphabetical order

  • Bearz – A Bear creature evolved from Catboy's favourite potato snack
  • Boobee - Catboy's obsession and main food source - pops up many times even if inconvenient and unexpected.
  • Cat-boy Castle– Catboy’s base of operations
  • Catboy – The Hero transformed by the toddler that winks his eyes at the sign of danger boasting two legs and six arms makes him a tour de force.
  • Catdog – The magic that made cat dog created a cat and a dog creature who guard cat castle.
  • Daah – A great wizard that Created Catboy with the help of his partner Mar-me-duc
  • Dar-bin – A powerful warrior gifted with great magic one of Catboy’s many helpers
  • Frog – Aids the hero during water exercises. Very old and wise but not great in a battle
  • Geko – The tree and land dweller and student of Frog likes to tickle his own feet to this allows him to make magic.
  • Ghost - Actually a statue of the naked Venus however, this is a game for children so not clear by the image. According to Catboy she comes to life at night.
  • Glammy – A guardian of Catboy and also known as mar-mars a powerful and good witch. Accidently turned Mar-me into the half duck human she is.
  • Grand - A Siamese slug monster twins but with only one head and the additional brain making him very powerful. He plays both side off on each other
  • Kay-kee – Another staple in Catboy’s diet which evolved into an army of gingerbread men. There are so many it’s easier just to call them all Kay-kee coming from the word cake.
  • Karr – A villain and a product of magic he tried to give Catboy a hard time.
  • Knotting Mouth – A powerful Nemesis – who was once sedated to stitch his mouth up to stop him eating people and casting spells.
  • Mar-me-duc – Catboy’s alter ego Winky-face main protector who is actually a fox and not a cat
  • Moo – Once a normal Bull on the Farm again all this chaotic magic flying around caused this friendly monstrosity sporting his own six pack. A friend of Viking Peet.
  • Mook – The magical chalice that provides sustenance to Catboy.
  • Mum-mers – Protector of Glammy who over sees Mar-me-duc. Their strength combined aids Catboy and occasional kits him out with different costumes.
  • Oppih - Is a magical over weight unicorn hippopotamus, a magical creature protective of Catboy.
  • Pee-Peels – Ancient creature who absorbs the souls of Evil people to use agains Catboy.
  • Peet – A Viking who feel back through time during an incident would you believe it involving magic.
  • Raaaa – A Blue Dinosaur who Catboy rides into battle – It is believed he is the result of magic also after Peet and Dar-bin attended a Ray Harryhausen exhibition drunk.
  • Rory Dragon – When Mum-mers is enraged she can transform to a dragon of pure rage however she has a tendency of attacking her allies in error
  • T-Ruck – A squid that likes punch-ups and dwells inland serving Pee-Peels, Karr, and Knotting-mouth
  • Twoo – A wise Owl who is caretaker over the Library of magical books within Cat-Castle…clearly, he has a lot to answer for with all this misplaced magic.
  • Upsteps – A large creature working with the forces of evil his downfall his stair legs can be climbed to box his ears.
  • Weewoo – A very vocal cat who sleeps a lot a friend of Catboy found by Mar-me-duc and cared for by Mum-mers, Dar-bin and Peet during various missions
  • Whistle – Karr’s righthand man, muscle for hire
  • Winky Face – This is Catboy prior to changing from 2 year old to get adult like warrior who is rather well behaved considering his powers.
